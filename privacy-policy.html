Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Window Seat AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a flight companion app developed by Renatas Stonkus. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Location Data

  • The app uses your device's GPS to determine your position during flights and show nearby landmarks.
  • Location data is processed on your device only. We do not transmit your GPS coordinates to our servers.
  • You can deny location permission and still use the app in demo/manual mode.

API Keys You Provide

  • The app allows you to enter your own API keys (e.g., ElevenLabs for narration, Google Maps) in Settings.
  • These keys are stored locally on your device only. We never collect, transmit, or store your API keys on any server.

Offline Data

  • The app downloads map tiles and landmark data for offline use during flights.
  • This data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not track your browsing activity or app usage analytics.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or ad tracking.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may connect to the following third-party services when you choose to use them:

  • Google Maps API — for map display and landmark data. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs API — for AI voice narration (only if you provide your own API key). Subject to ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-generated landmark descriptions (only if you provide your own API key). Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

These services are only accessed when you actively use features that require them. No data is sent to these services without your action.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All user data (settings, API keys, cached maps) is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate servers that store user data.
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security (encryption, screen lock, etc.).

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access & Delete: Clear app data or uninstall the app at any time to remove all stored data.
  • Permissions: Revoke location or other permissions through your device settings at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
