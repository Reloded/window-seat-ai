Privacy Policy

Window Seat AI

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Overview

Window Seat AI ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Flight Information

  • Flight numbers you search for
  • This data is used solely to retrieve route information and is not stored on our servers

API Keys (Optional)

  • If you provide your own API keys (Claude, ElevenLabs), they are stored locally on your device only
  • We never transmit your API keys to our servers

Device Information

  • Basic device info for crash reporting and app improvement
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data (GPS)
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Contacts or photos
  • Browsing history

Third-Party Services

The App uses these third-party services:

  • AeroAPI (FlightAware) — For flight route data
  • Anthropic Claude — For generating narration content
  • ElevenLabs — For text-to-speech (optional)
  • MapTiler — For offline map tiles

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Downloaded flight packs
  • Cached map tiles
  • Audio narration files
  • Your preferences and settings

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the App and on our website.
